About Me

With a diverse background spanning over three decades, Steven Nosack has cultivated a career marked by service, leadership, and community engagement.

Having spent 11 years as a military police officer, Steven honed skills in patrol operations, traffic accident investigation, and military police investigation, rising to the rank of Operations NCO. Transitioning from the military, Steven embarked on a 15-year journey in the trucking industry, amassing an impressive two and a half million miles of safe driving across the contiguous United States. Recognized for exemplary performance, Steven was awarded the prestigious title of Utah Trucking Association's Driver of the Month in February 2011.

In pursuit of enhancing safety standards within the transportation sector, Steven transitioned into a leadership role as a safety manager for a regional carrier specializing in perishable groceries and produce. Over the course of a decade, Steven ascended to the position of Operations Manager, directly reporting to the company's president, and spearheaded initiatives to optimize operational efficiency and ensure regulatory compliance.

Beyond the realm of transportation, Steven is deeply entrenched in civic engagement, actively participating in local and state political campaigns. This passion for community service culminated in Steven's appointment as a Town Councilmember, where strategic decision-making and advocacy for constituents became paramount. Additionally, Steven served as Vice President of the local Chamber of Commerce, leveraging expertise in event planning and management to orchestrate successful initiatives.

Renowned for organizational prowess, Steven assumed the role of Chairman for the town's iconic Fourth of July celebration, orchestrating a day-long extravaganza featuring a morning pancake breakfast, a 5K run, a storied parade, and culminating in a grand concert and fireworks display. Furthermore, Steven acquired the Halloween in Summer Festival, a beloved community event drawing thousands of visitors annually, and transformed it into a cultural phenomenon. Held on Historic Main Street, the festival boasted a myriad of attractions, including over 120 vendors, captivating entertainment, delectable cuisine, and a spirited cosplay contest, culminating in an unforgettable after-party experience.

In addition to his multifaceted career, Steven has been a licensed Realtor since 1996, transitioning to full-time real estate in 2014. His dedication, innovation, and excellence have left an indelible mark on the communities and industries fortunate enough to intersect with his remarkable journey.
